BECOME A MENTOR

Greetings (Future) Mentors!

Shape young minds. Meet hackers. Tour museums. Free t-shirt.

Hack the Future is always looking for qualified mentors. If you have experience kid-wrangling, programming, or both, we'd love to have you join the group. You can decide your level of involvement, from just listening in on group emails, helping run the event, or helping plan in any level of detail you like.

And good news for amnesiacs! Free background check.

The structure of the event is a freeform hackathon. For those who want somewhere to start, there are stations where an open-ended workshop is provided. Their goal is to give future hackers a gentle shove down the path of self-teaching, so they should be interesting, should be very open-ended, and a participant should be able to continue the activity when they get home. We're always in need of volunteers to create, help run, and suggest stations. In particular, we need women technologiests to help mentor female future hackers.

We communicate via an email list and at several in-person meetups prior to an event.
