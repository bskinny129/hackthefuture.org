BECOME A MENTOR

Greetings (Future) Mentors!

Hack the Future is always looking for qualified mentors. If you have experience kid-wrangling, programming, or both, we'd love to have you join the group. You can decide your level of involvement, from just listening in on group emails, helping run the event, or helping plan in any level of detail you like.

Perhaps one of the most important items to get in order are the stations. The structure of the event is a set of stations where an activity is provided. Ideally this activity is open-ended and can be continued later at home by a Future Hacker. Our goal is to give them a gentle nudge in the direction of teaching themselves technology and have them take over on their own.

We're always in need of volunteers to create, help run, and suggest stations. In particular, we have a need for women technologiests to help mentor female future hackers.

We communicate via an email list and at several in-person meetups prior to an event. These meetups often happen at DevHouse, the progenitor of Hack the Future. Contact us if you're interested.
