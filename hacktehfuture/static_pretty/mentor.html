BECOME A MENTOR

Greetings (Future) Mentors!

Shape young minds. Meet hackers. Tour museums. Free t-shirt. Build your professional network. Maybe even get a job. Yes, people have gotten jobs by volunteering at Hack the Future. And if that fails, free t-shirt.

Hack the Future is always looking for qualified mentors. If you have experience kid-wrangling, programming, or both, we'd love to have you join the group. You can decide your level of involvement, from just listening in on group emails, helping run the event, or helping plan in any level of detail you like.

The structure of the event is a set of stations where an activity is provided. Each activity is an open-ended workshop inteded to get a student interested enough to continue on their own. We're always in need of volunteers to create, help run, and suggest stations. In particular, we need women technologiests to help mentor female future hackers.

We communicate via an email list and at several in-person meetups prior to an event.
